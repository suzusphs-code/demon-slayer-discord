§ 01 Overview

Demon Slayer RPG Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of all users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you interact with the Bot, how we use it, how it is stored, and what rights you have over your data.

✓ Our Commitment

We collect only the data strictly necessary to operate the Bot. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We never will.

By using the Bot, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the Bot and request deletion of your data by contacting our support server.

§ 02 What We Collect

We collect and store the following categories of data:

Data Type What It Is Why We Need It
Discord User ID Your unique Discord account number To identify your account in our database and link all your game data to you
Discord Username Your username as it appears on Discord To display your name in leaderboards and embeds
Game Progress Characters, levels, currencies, team, inventory, fragments To save and retrieve your in-game state
Battle Records Win/loss history, PvP stats, kill streak To display rankings and track achievements
Transaction History In-game purchases (coins, gems), pull history To maintain economy integrity and resolve disputes
Command Usage Which commands you run and when To detect abuse, debug issues, and improve features
Guild/Server ID The Discord server where you used the Bot To manage server-specific raid and guild war settings
Premium Status Whether you have an active Patreon subscription To apply Premium benefits to your account
ℹ What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your email address, phone number, real name, IP address, payment card details, messages sent in Discord channels (other than commands directed at the Bot), or any data from Discord DMs unless you directly message the Bot a command.

§ 03 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate, maintain, and improve the Bot. Specifically:

  • To provide core Bot functionality — saving your characters, balance, team, and progress between sessions.
  • To power leaderboards, rankings, and social features such as guild wars and raid participation.
  • To enforce fair play — detecting and acting on exploitation, multi-accounting, and abuse.
  • To apply Premium benefits to accounts that have an active subscription.
  • To debug errors, investigate reports, and improve the Bot's stability and features.
  • To send important bot-related announcements via our support server (never unsolicited DMs).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the Bot.

§ 04 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a MongoDB database hosted on secured cloud infrastructure. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

  • Database access is restricted to authorised developers only.
  • No plaintext passwords are stored — we rely on Discord's own authentication.
  • Regular backups are performed to prevent data loss.
ℹ Note on Security

No system is 100% secure. While we take data protection seriously, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet. In the event of a data breach we will notify affected users via our Discord support server as promptly as possible.

§ 05 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Period.

We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Our hosting and database providers process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements. They are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
  • Patreon: When you subscribe to Premium via Patreon, Patreon shares your Patreon user ID and subscription status with us to verify your tier. We receive no payment details. Patreon's own privacy policy governs what they collect.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Safety: We may share data to protect the safety of our users or the public where we believe there is a serious risk of harm.

§ 06 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you are an active user of the Bot, or as long as necessary to provide the service. Specifically:

  • Active accounts are retained indefinitely to preserve your game progress.
  • Accounts with no activity for more than 12 consecutive months may be purged at our discretion.
  • Command usage logs are retained for up to 90 days for debugging and anti-abuse purposes.
  • Upon a verified deletion request, your data will be removed within 30 days.

§ 07 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restriction: You may request we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
✓ How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit any data request, join our support server at discord.gg/gGkMq8y43e and open a support ticket. We will respond within 30 days. Please note that deleting your data will permanently erase all in-game progress and cannot be undone.

§ 08 Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under 13 years of age, consistent with Discord's minimum age requirement. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to the Bot, please contact us via our support server.

§ 09 Third-Party Services

The Bot integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Discord: The platform on which the Bot operates. Discord collects its own data in accordance with its Privacy Policy.
  • Patreon: Used for Premium subscription management. Governed by Patreon's Privacy Policy.
  • Top.gg: We submit server count statistics to Top.gg for bot listing purposes. No personal user data is shared. Governed by Top.gg's Privacy Policy.
  • MongoDB Atlas / Hosting Provider: Cloud infrastructure used to store game data. Processes data only as instructed by us.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their policies independently.

§ 10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and announce the update in our support server.

Your continued use of the Bot after any changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy. If you do not agree with the revised Policy, you may request deletion of your data and stop using the Bot.
